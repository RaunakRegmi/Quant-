## Stage 1: Heavy Foundation (Mathematics & Logic)

Mathematics is the core of quantitative finance. Without strong fundamentals, programming and models are meaningless.

### Probability & Statistics
Key topics:
- Conditional Probability
- Bayes’ Theorem
- Combinatorics
- Random Variables
- Probability Distributions
- Expectation & Variance
- PMF, PDF, CDF

Recommended textbook:
- *Introduction to Probability* — Dimitri Bertsekas

Importance:
Used for modeling uncertainty in financial markets and risk.

---

### Linear Algebra
Key topics:
- Vectors and Matrices
- Matrix Multiplication
- Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors
- Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)
- Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

Recommended textbook:
- *Linear Algebra and Its Applications* — Gilbert Strang

Importance:
Used for dimensionality reduction, portfolio optimization, and financial data modeling.

---

### Mental Math & Problem Solving
Practice tools:
- Zetamac
- Trader mental math drills

Reference:
- *A Practical Guide to Quantitative Finance Interviews* — Xinfeng Zhou

Importance:
Improves speed and accuracy in calculations under time pressure.
